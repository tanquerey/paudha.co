Blending the science of horticulture with the craft of architecture

At Paudha, we believe that your quality of life can be improved by fusing the natural world into your house, workplace, or outdoor spaces. We are stylists, horticulturalists, and plant enthusiasts with the aim of taking the beauty of nature indoors to make more convenient, appealing, and productive places to live, work and play.

What we do ?

We build living installations that cultivate a bond with nature,
bringing the essence of greenery to various homes in Delhi
NCR. Paudha fills the divide between plants and individuals,
delivering goods and services that fit your personal
requirements, lifestyle, and budget. To plant enthusiasts, we
give a tried and tested range of seeds, planters, decorations,
horticultural information, and unlimited quality designs to
revamp your homes and lives. In attempts of merging the
beauty of the outside world with indoor spaces, we specialize in
providing you a comfortable and cozy area to unwind and
enjoy. Paudha is enthusiastic about biophilic nature and aims to
elevate traditional conceptions of how full human comfort and
well-being can be improved with a creative amalgamation of
our environment..

Who we are?

Paudha is here to deepen your relationship with plants and nature as a whole. We are a team of plant-passionate individuals, working together to express our conviction towards the notion – “the quality of life can be enhanced with an abundance of plants in our surroundings” Plants create an earthly and natural vibe, and they sustain our lives. Together with Paudha, let’s trengthen our relationship with nature and return the delightful favor.

Why are we doing it?

Plants make us happier, healthier, more efficient, and boost our creativity. They also purify the air around us, making our environment cleaner and safer. The modern lifestyle has us spending most of our daily lives indoors, deprived of the beauty of nature. Hoping to initiate a positive change in this lifestyle, Paudha believes that everyone should have a little green in their lives, regardless of their location. Paudha focuses on the emerging field of biophilic design and working with clients to create environments that integrate nature into human spaces successfully, making our homes livelier, healthier, and happier. Plants are divine organic beings that must be treated with care and love. They are not be disposed of or neglected in broad daylight. Promoting the same, and upholding the virtues of nature preservation and reverence for botanical life, we promote the plant movement and believe that a little green goes a long way in our lives.
