Virtual Design consultation

As we say, well planned is half done. For those of you who like
to take matters in your own hand, we have this virtual design consultation service in which
we design, you create. This services gives you the joy of creating your own garden while
ensuring that you plan it well. We help from planning and designing to execution and
maintenance. We will share with you ideas and concept plans so you can make your own
space. Design will include 2D and 3D renders. As part of online consultation, we will advice
you on the type of plants and materials(planters, seating, flooring, lighting etc to use, based
on the climate conditions in your region. You will also have an option to buy best quality
materials from us as per the proposed design. This service is available for customers all over
India.
